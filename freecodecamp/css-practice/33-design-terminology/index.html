Layout

Fundamental

Layout is how visual elements are arranged on a page to communicate a message. It's the "blueprint" of your design, considering placement, size, and hierarchy.

Structure

Alignment creates visual order by placing elements in relation to one another along imaginary lines or edges.

Center Aligned
