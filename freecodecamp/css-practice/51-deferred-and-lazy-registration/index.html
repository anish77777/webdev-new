Deferred & Lazy Registration

Don't stop your users at the door. Let them experience the value of your application before asking for their information.

üíé Show Value First

Users need to see what your site offers before they're willing to register. Think of an e-commerce site: let them browse products and add items to a cart freely. Only ask for registration when they're ready to purchase.
