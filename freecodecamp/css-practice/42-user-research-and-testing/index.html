Research, Testing & Requirements

Systematic study and data-driven decisions for building products that people actually want and love to use.

User Research

User research is the systematic study of the people who use your product. The goal is to measure user needs, behaviors, and pain points.

Net Promoter Score (NPS)

NPS measures how likely users are to recommend your product. It is measured on a scale of 0 to 10. Promoters (9-10) are your biggest fans.

User Requirements

Requirements define the rubric your application must follow. They can be functional (how it works) or non-functional (how it behaves).

Functional

"User can add item to cart"
"Admin can delete user accounts"
"Search bar must return results"

Non-Functional

"Page must load in < 2s"
"Must support high-contrast mode"
"App should handle 10k users"
