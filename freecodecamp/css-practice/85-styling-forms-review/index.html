Best Practices for Styling Inputs

Accessibility is the cornerstone of form design. When styling text inputs, ensure:

  • Adequate Sizing: Fonts should be large enough for easy reading.
  • Sufficient Contrast: Text must stand out clearly against the background.
  • Focus Indicators: Always preserve a noticeable indicator (like a bold border) when an element is focused.
