The WCAG Standards

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) provide benchmarks for contrast. Every designer and developer should aim for these ratios:

AA Standard (4.5:1)

The minimum requirement for body text.

Accessible Body Text

AAA Standard (7:1)

The highest level of accessibility.

Maximum Readability

The Impact of Opacity

Opacity (the alpha channel) can bridge background and foreground. However, lowering opacity often reduces contrast because it blends the text into the background color.

100% Opacity

Crispy and clear text.

50% Opacity

Fading into the background.

20% Opacity

Nearly invisible / Low Accessibility.
