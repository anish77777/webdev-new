3. Consistency & Limit

Use two or three fonts at most. Using too many can weaken brand identity and increase page load times.

Tip: Pick one font for headings and another for body text. Maintain this across all pages.

4. Accessibility (WCAG)

Ensure high contrast between text and background for users with visual disabilities.

High Contrast

Easy to read: White text on dark background (#e2e8f0 on #0a0c10)

Low Contrast

Difficult to read: Gray text on slate background (#475569 on #1e293b)
